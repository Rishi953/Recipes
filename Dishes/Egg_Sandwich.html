Egg Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped
  • Mayonnaise
  • sauces any which you have(optional)
  • Salt and pepper as per taste
  • Pizza Seasoning
  • 4 Slices Bread

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Add 2 spoon of mayonnaise,salt,pepper and pizza Seasoning and Mix it well then add the egg and mix it.
  3. If you have sauce then apply on the slice of bread
  4. Divide the filling equally between the 2 slices of bread, top with remainig slices

Tips

  • you can cook bread too.
  • Pair with schezwan chutney.
