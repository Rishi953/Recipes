White Sauce Pasta

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Milk
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper as per taste
  • Pizza Seasoning
  • All Purpose Flour

Directions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until soft. Drain and keep aside.
  2. In a pan, heat butter or oil on low flame.
  3. Add flour (maida) and stir continuously for 1–2 minutes — don’t let it brown.
  4. Slowly pour in milk, stirring constantly to avoid lumps.
  5. Once the sauce thickens, add salt (and pepper if you have it.
  6. Add boiled pasta to the sauce, mix well, and cook for 1–2 minutes.

Tips

  • Stir milk slowly to avoid lumps in the sauce.
  • Don't overcook the pasta.
