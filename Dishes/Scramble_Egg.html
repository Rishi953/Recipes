Scrambled Egg

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pizza Seasoning (optional)

Directions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat them well with a fork or spoon..
  2. Heat a pan on medium flame and add oil or butter.
  3. Pour in the beaten eggs.
  4. Let it sit for a few seconds, then gently stir it

  6. Add salt (and pizza seasoning if using) at the end and mix.
